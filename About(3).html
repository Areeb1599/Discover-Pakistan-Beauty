About Us


Our Story

Discover Pakistan is one of the first of the new generation adventure and tourism companies in Pakistan which breathed a new life in the domestic tourism industry. We are a group of young adventurers with a passion to discover and explore. Discover Pakistan holds expertise in Rock Climbing, Trekking, Hiking, Camping, Paragliding, Mountaineering & Ice-Climbing, Rafting and Skiing. With committed professionals Discover Pakistan aspires to provide quality services to our valued adventurers. We believe that quality in a service or sports is not what you put into it, it is what our cherished discoverers gets out of it.






Vision

Our vision is to be more than a number 1 Tours and Adventure Sports Company that not only provide the best services to adventure lovers under one umbrella but a unique experience of sharing bonds with our valued participants.








Mission

Our mission is to provide everyone from first-time participants to professionals with the best of equipments, cooperation and fair chance of cooperative learning to all. We are determined to continue our engagement with the people with positive travel experiences delivered with a sagacity of tenderness, affability, and entity pride and group fortitude.
