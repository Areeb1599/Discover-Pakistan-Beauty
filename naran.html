Naran Valley



Naran Valley is often mistaken as part of Skardu, but it is actually located in the upper Kaghan Valley within Mansehra District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Situated at 2,409 meters (7,904 feet) above sea level, Naran offers a cool retreat from the summer heat.
